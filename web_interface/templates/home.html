ElderSafe Connect

Welcome to ElderSafe Connect, your all-in-one incident management hub for aged-care facilities. Below are just a few of our key capabilities:

🔔 Real-Time Alerts

Instant notifications for security incidents, suspicious logins, or system anomalies—so you can act before small issues become big problems.

🛠️ Admin Dashboard

Manage residents and staff in one place: add new residents, generate staff invites with specific roles, view all users and residents at a glance, and access collapsible logs for login, JWT, and honeypot events.

👥 Role-Based Access

Securely manage staff permissions—admins, nurses, carers—so everyone sees exactly what they need, nothing more, nothing less.

🔒 Integrated MFA

Protect sensitive resident data with built-in multi-factor authentication powered by Supabase, ensuring only authorized personnel can log in.

📁 Secure Data Storage

Encrypted backups and automated versioning ensure all resident and incident data remains safe, intact, and recoverable at any time.

📱 Mobile-Friendly

Responsive design and mobile-optimized views let caregivers and administrators access the portal seamlessly on tablets or smartphones.
